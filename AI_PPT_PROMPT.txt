- To establish Kerala as a model for digital governance in technical education

### Slide 4: Mission Objectives

3. Provide real-time tracking and analytics for informed decision-making

5. Create a scalable system capable of handling future growth
6. Establish accountability through comprehensive activity logging

## SECTION 2: PROBLEM ANALYSIS

### Slide 5: Current Landscape - The Challenge
- 60 polytechnic colleges across Kerala
- Approximately 20,000 students graduating annually
- Each college managing placements independently
- No centralized view of placement activities across the state
- Inconsistent processes and varying standards

### Slide 6: Problem Statement - Fragmented Systems
- Each of the 60 colleges operates its own placement process
- No standardized approach across institutions
- Duplication of efforts and resources
- Companies must coordinate with multiple colleges separately
- No mechanism for cross-college opportunity sharing

### Slide 7: Problem Statement - Manual Processes
- Paper-based student registrations causing delays
- Physical file management leading to data loss risks
- Manual eligibility verification prone to errors
- Time-consuming report generation
- Difficulty in maintaining updated student records

### Slide 8: Problem Statement - Limited Visibility
- Education department lacks centralized placement data
- Difficult to assess statewide placement performance
- No real-time insights for policy decisions
- Historical data scattered across colleges
- Challenging to identify trends and patterns

### Slide 9: Problem Statement - Student Disadvantages
- Students unaware of opportunities at other colleges
- Limited access based on college location and connections
- No transparency in selection processes
- Difficulty tracking application status
- Missed opportunities due to information gaps

### Slide 10: Problem Statement - Administrative Burden
- Placement officers overwhelmed with manual tasks
- Repetitive data entry across multiple systems
- Difficulty generating accurate reports
- No tools for efficient student management
- Limited capacity to handle increasing student numbers

### Slide 11: Impact of Current Challenges
- Lower placement rates than potential
- Unequal opportunity distribution across colleges
- Delayed decision-making due to data unavailability
- Student dissatisfaction with placement services
- Inefficient use of administrative resources
- Difficulty attracting companies due to fragmented approach

## SECTION 4: USER ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES

| Role | Users | Primary Function |

### Slide 17: Student Role - Overview
- Approximately 20,000 students across 60 colleges
- Self-service registration and profile management
- Access to all eligible job opportunities across the state
- Real-time application tracking and notifications
- Digital placement journey from registration to final outcome

### Slide 18: Student Role - Registration Process
1. Enter Permanent Registration Number (PRN) for validation
2. Select Region, College, and Branch from dropdown menus
3. Provide basic personal information
4. Upload passport-size photograph
5. Create secure password
6. Verify email address through confirmation link
7. Submit registration for Placement Officer approval

### Slide 19: Student Role - Profile System
**Tier 1: Basic Profile**
- Personal details: Name, date of birth, contact information
- Academic information: College, branch, admission year
- Current semester and CGPA
- Document uploads: Resume, certificates

**Tier 2: Extended Profile**
- Family information and background
- Class 10th and 12th marks
- Semester-wise academic performance
- Career preferences and location preferences

**Tier 3: Custom Fields**
- Job-specific information requested by companies
- Professional certifications
- Technical skills and competencies
- Extracurricular activities and achievements

### Slide 20: Student Role - Job Application Journey
1. View all available jobs on personal dashboard
2. System automatically checks eligibility based on profile
3. If profile incomplete, guided prompts to complete required fields
4. Apply to eligible positions with one click
5. Track application through status stages
6. Receive email notifications at each stage
7. View final outcome: Selected or Not Selected

### Slide 21: Student Application Status Flow
[Create a simple flow diagram:]
- Submitted → Under Review → Shortlisted → Selected/Rejected
- Each stage clearly defined with visual progression
- Show that students can track their position in real-time

### Slide 22: Placement Officer Role - Overview
- One designated officer per college (60 total)
- Acts as the college-level administrator
- Bridge between students and state administration
- Responsible for all placement activities within their college
- Reports to Super Admin for approvals and compliance

### Slide 23: Placement Officer Role - Dashboard
- At-a-glance view of college placement status
- Pending student approvals requiring action
- Active jobs available for students
- Recent placement statistics
- Quick access to all management functions
- Notifications and alerts for time-sensitive actions

### Slide 24: Placement Officer Role - Student Management
- View complete list of registered students
- Filter and search by various criteria
- Approve or reject new registrations
- Review and verify student profiles
- Blacklist students for policy violations
- Request whitelist for rehabilitation cases
- Export student lists to PDF or Excel formats

### Slide 25: Placement Officer Role - Job Coordination
- Request new job postings from Super Admin
- Specify complete company and job details
- Define eligibility criteria: CGPA cutoff, allowed backlogs, eligible branches
- Configure which profile fields are required for application
- Track approval status of submitted requests
- View and manage approved jobs for their college

### Slide 26: Placement Officer Role - Placement Recording
- View all applicants for each job
- Update application statuses as process progresses
- Record final placement outcomes
- Enter placement details: Package, location, joining date
- Generate placement reports for college records
- Export data for department submissions

### Slide 27: Super Administrator Role - Overview
- State-level system administrators
- Complete oversight of all 60 colleges
- Ultimate authority for job approvals
- System-wide configuration and management
- Access to comprehensive analytics and reports
- Ensures policy compliance across all institutions

### Slide 28: Super Administrator Role - Dashboard
- Statewide placement statistics at a glance
- Performance metrics across all 60 colleges
- Pending approvals requiring attention
- System health and activity overview
- Quick access to administrative functions
- Alerts for issues requiring intervention

### Slide 29: Super Administrator Role - College Management
- View and manage all 60 polytechnic colleges
- Create and manage Placement Officer accounts
- Reset credentials and manage access
- Monitor college-level activity
- Ensure compliance with system policies
- Address escalated issues from colleges

### Slide 30: Super Administrator Role - Job Management
- Review and approve/reject job requests from officers
- Create jobs applicable to multiple colleges simultaneously
- Configure statewide job postings
- Set system-wide eligibility standards
- Manage company templates for recurring recruiters
- Ensure quality and legitimacy of job postings

### Slide 31: Super Administrator Role - System Control
- Manage PRN (Permanent Registration Number) ranges
- Configure system-wide settings
- Send notifications to all users or specific groups
- View complete activity logs for audit purposes
- Access comprehensive system reports
- Manage system security and access controls

### Slide 33: Registration System - PRN Validation
- Students must enter valid PRN to begin registration
- System validates PRN against authorized database
- Prevents unauthorized registrations
- Ensures only genuine polytechnic students can access the system
- PRN ranges managed by Super Admin per college

### Slide 34: Registration System - Email Verification
- Two-factor verification: PRN + Email
- Confirmation link sent to student's email
- Account activated only after email verification
- Ensures valid contact information for future communications
- Prevents fake or duplicate accounts

### Slide 35: Registration System - Approval Workflow
- Student submits registration with required details
- Placement Officer receives notification
- Officer reviews submitted information
- Officer verifies student identity and eligibility
- Registration approved or rejected with reason
- Student notified of decision via email

### Slide 36: Profile System - Three-Tier Architecture
